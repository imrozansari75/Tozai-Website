GENERAL PURPOSE

Our General Purpose gloves are designed for a wide range of applications, from heavy industrial work and assembly lines to serving as liners underneath other protective gloves. These gloves come in both knitted and cut-and-sewn varieties, offering excellent dexterity and flexibility. They are a cost-effective and versatile solution, available in various styles, fabrics, weights, colors, and sizes to meet the specific needs of different industries.
