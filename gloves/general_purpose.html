GENERAL PURPOSE

These knitted or cut and sewn gloves are used in a wide range of applications from heavy industrial work, assembly or even as liners underneath other gloves. Knit construction provides good dexterity. These are economical and versatile and available in different styles, weights and sizes for use in industries of various scales. Cut and sewn gloves can be woven or interlock. These are popular general application gloves made of various fabric qualities, weaves and weights. These are popular in all the industries.
